Our Interior Design Solutions

Carpenter Services

Our professional carpentry team specializes in designing and crafting premium-quality wooden furniture and interior elements that perfectly balance functionality and aesthetics. Whether it’s traditional craftsmanship or modern modular solutions, we bring your dream interiors to life with precision and detail.

  • Custom furniture design (sofas, tables, chairs, beds, etc.)
  • Modular kitchens with smart storage solutions
  • Wardrobes, cabinets, and shelving units
  • Wooden flooring and paneling
  • Office furniture & workspace interiors
  • Repair and restoration of wooden structures

Electrician Services

We provide end-to-end electrical solutions to make your interiors safe, energy-efficient, and modern. From new installations to advanced smart automation, our certified electricians ensure reliable service with high-quality materials and strict safety standards.

  • Electrical wiring & rewiring for homes and offices
  • Lighting design and installation (LEDs, chandeliers, ambient lights)
  • Power backup & UPS setup
  • Installation of fans, ACs, geysers, and other appliances
  • Smart home automation (voice control, IoT devices, sensors)
  • Electrical maintenance & troubleshooting
